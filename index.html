Introduction

Hello! we are the group 3 and are assigned to pitching and presentation for Technopreneurship!

We, the group 3, are tasked to create or present a software where we may apply the concept of pitching and presentation!

Are you familiar with this show?

Are you familiar with these people?

"This is "Shark Tank". It is a TV show where entrepreneurs pitch their business ideas to investors (the "sharks") to get funding in exchange for a share of their company. It's known for showcasing a variety of products and services and for the intense negotiations that happen on the show.

Here is one of the episodes of the Shark Tank:

In order to apply this concept, we needed to create or choose a product to pitch.

Thus, the Group 3 has choosen the option to choose a product from the internet assuming it is the product we created to to pitch and present!

In this case, it is considered as a school activity, and since the software we have found is not ours, we will not involve any money. But will carry out the concept of Pitching and Preentation!

Instalacarte.

As we have took the option to find a software, we have searched the internet and found Instalacarte!

instalacarte is a QR code ordering system for restaurants and cafe.

Try and checkout this cool application here:

It allows restaurant or cafe owners to create and customize their own QR code ordering system.

Now that we have a software, we must come up with a plan on how can we pitch and present it!

Preparation.

In order to carry out a successful pitch, we must do market research, identifying the business problem, business solution and emphasize the target market!

We did our research that supports the implmentation of QR Code ordering systems.

Aside from doing our research we also needed to analyze our business proposal and to be creative on how can we deliver it.

We conducted online meetings to think of a business strategy.



With the studies we used and conducted, we have identified the business problem.

We then Identfied the business model to be used and costs that needed Instalacarte to grow
