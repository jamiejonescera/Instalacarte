Brainstorm Part 1

As a team, we have chosen cafe and restaurant infrastructures to observe, what are the common problems they encounter.


And by this way, we may brainstorm what technology and application can be suitable for their business problem.


Business Problem

Order process deals with how customers make order by looking at menus, and assuring what they want to order, and sometimes they deal with standing on a line to do their order. Business Solution

We propose a QR Code Ordering system, that has a high chance in reducing the business problem and upgrading the ordering system of a cafe and restuarant. We believed that this solution would work, just needed to conduct a study to prove and present it to the investors.

Instalacarte.

Ladies and Gentleman! We present the QR Code ordering system of the future!

Instalacarte

Instalacarte is a QR code ordering system for restaurants and cafes.

It allows restaurant or cafe owners to create and customize their own QR code ordering system and can assign it on a table.



Features of Instalacarte



The Instalacarte is good and running, and we were planning to launch the application by August 2024. And as a technopreneur, we used a subscription model to generate revenue for Instalacarte of a monthly income of P 1,100.00.



The good thing here is that, upon our early promotion there are already 7 cafe and restaurant owners, like Cafe Roo QC that want to subscribe at the launch month.



Web Application Problem: Finances

However, just like every entrepreneurs problem in the Shark Tank show, there were these developments that required, finances, that we as students hardly cannot afford. Since this is a big project and application, we needed to host, test (for ready use) and promote the application.

The required expense for Instalacarte to develop has amounted to P 35,800.00, thus we needed Angel Investors, Business Communities, business man and woman who would like to help us and invest in this web application software and goal.
