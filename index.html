Instalacarte.

Ladies and Gentleman! We present the QR Code ordering system of the future!

Instalacarte

Instalacarte is a QR code ordering system for restaurants and cafes.

It allows restaurant or cafe owners to create and customize their own QR code ordering system and can assign it on a table.



Features of Instalacarte



The Instalacarte is good and running, and we were planning to launch the application by August 2024. And as a technopreneur, we used a subscription model to generate revenue for Instalacarte of a monthly income of P 1,100.00.



The good thing here is that, upon our early promotion there are already 7 cafe and restaurant owners, like Cafe Roo QC that want to subscribe at the launch month.
